Game of Life

How to Play

  • Click and drag on the grid to paint live cells
  • Play to start the simulation
  • Pause then Step Forward to advance one generation at a time
  • Shuffle to create a random pattern
  • Reset to clear the grid

About

Conway's Game of Life is a cellular automaton where simple rules determine whether cells live or die each generation:

  • A live cell with 2-3 neighbors survives
  • A dead cell with exactly 3 neighbors comes alive
  • All other cells die or stay dead

Troubleshooting

Requirements

WebGPU must be enabled in your browser. Use a recent version of Chrome, Safari, or Firefox.

iOS Safari

Go to Settings → Apps → Safari → Advanced → Feature Flags → enable "WebGPU"

Performance

This simulation runs entirely on your GPU using Rust and WebGPU for optimal performance.
